Postgraduate Diploma in Applied Counselling Psychology (PGDipACP)

Designed for individuals seeking to deepen their understanding of human behaviour, mental health, and therapeutic practices while gaining practical skills to support clients in diverse settings. The programme integrates evidence-based theory with real-world application, preparing graduates to work as counsellors, psychotherapists, or mental health practitioners in Singapore's multicultural society.

Fee: S$14,550
Intake: 1 Jan 2026 – 31 Dec 2026 · 50 sessions
Schedule: Thursdays, 7:00 pm, 3 hours
